Our Mission

The mission of the Publishing Working Group is to enable all publications — with all their specificities and traditions — to become first-class entities on the Web. The WG will provide the necessary technologies on the Open Web Platform to make the combination of traditional publishing and the Web complete in terms of accessibility, usability, portability, distribution, archiving, offline access, and reliable cross referencing.

The Co-Chairs of the Working Group are Tzviya Siegman (Wiley) and Garth Conboy (Google). The W3C Staff Contact for the Working Group is Ivan Herman.

Group Communications

Mailing lists

  • public-publ-wg@w3.org: the group’s main mailing list. Group members are automatically signed up. The archives are publicly visible, but anyone can post a mail.
  • member-publ-wg@w3.org: the group’s member confidential mailing list. Group members are automatically signed up. The archives are visible to W3C members only, and only group members can post a mail.
  • group-publ-wg-chairs@w3.org: list for chairs and W3C staff contact(s). The Archives are only visible to the chairs and the W3C staff, but anybody can post a mail.
